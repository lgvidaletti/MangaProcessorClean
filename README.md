# MangaProcessorClean
xes what KCC, Kindle Comic Converter and Mapaki simply can't — manga with images smaller than your Kindle Scribe's 300 DPI screen. Upscales every page with Lanczos, auto-detects orientation &amp; background color, corrects washed-out grays, and runs in parallel. Because you've always deserved to see your manga looking absolutely stunning.
